Privacy Policy

This application (hereinafter referred to as "the App") recognizes the importance of protecting users' personal information and complies with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (hereinafter referred to as the "Personal Information Protection Act"). We strive for the appropriate handling and protection of personal information in accordance with the following privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Privacy Policy").

Article 1 (Definition of Personal Information)

In this Privacy Policy, personal information is defined as personal information under Article 2, Paragraph 1 of the Personal Information Protection Act. This refers to information about a living individual that can identify a specific individual by name, date of birth, or other descriptions contained in such information (including information that can be easily collated with other information, thereby identifying a specific individual), or information containing an individual identification code.

Article 2 (Purpose of Use of Personal Information)

The App will use users' personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the services of the App.
  • To provide guidance and respond to inquiries regarding the App.
  • To respond to acts that violate our terms, policies, etc. (hereinafter referred to as "Terms, etc.") regarding the App.
  • To notify users of changes to the Terms, etc. regarding the App.
  • To analyze and improve the usage of the App.
  • To create statistical data related to the use of the App.
  • For other tasks necessary for the operation of the App.

Article 3 (Proper Acquisition of Personal Information)

The App will acquire users' personal information by appropriate means. When acquiring personal information from users, the App will specify the purpose of use and acquire personal information within the necessary scope.

Article 4 (Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties)

The App will not provide personal information to third parties without the user's consent. However, personal information may be provided to third parties without the user's consent in the following cases:

  • When required by law.
  • When it is necessary for the protection of a person's life, body, or property, and it is difficult to obtain the person's consent.
  • When it is particularly necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound upbringing of children, and it is difficult to obtain the person's consent.
  • When it is necessary to cooperate with a national agency, a local government, or a person entrusted by them in executing the affairs prescribed by laws and regulations, and obtaining the person's consent is likely to impede the execution of such affairs.

Article 5 (Management of Personal Information)

The App will strive to keep users' personal information accurate and up-to-date. The App will take necessary and appropriate security control measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, and leakage of users' personal information.

Article 6 (Disclosure, Correction, Suspension of Use, etc. of Personal Information)

Users may request the App to disclose, correct, add, delete, suspend the use of, erase, and stop the provision to third parties of their personal information (hereinafter referred to as "Disclosure, etc."). The App will respond appropriately to requests for Disclosure, etc. from users in accordance with the law.

Article 7 (Changes to the Privacy Policy)

The App may change this Privacy Policy due to amendments to laws and regulations, etc. If this Privacy Policy is changed, users will be notified by posting the revised content within the App.
