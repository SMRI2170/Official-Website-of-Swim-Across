Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General

Q1: What kind of app is "Swim Across"?

A1: "Swim Across" is an app that logs your daily swimming training and visualizes the total distance you've swum as a journey on a map. It's packed with features to keep you motivated, such as creating training menus, setting goal times, and managing your records on a calendar.

About the Map Feature

Q2: How do I set the start and end points on the map?

A2: On the "Map" screen, select the period you want to set (This Week, This Month, This Year). Then, long-press on the map to set the start point, and long-press again to set the end point. The distance of the set route automatically becomes your distance goal for that period.

Q3: What does my current location on the map represent?

A3: It represents your current position from the start point towards the end point, based on the swimming distance you've logged in the "Calendar".

About Logging and the Calendar

Q4: How do I log my training?

A4: From the "Add Record" button at the bottom right of the "Calendar" screen, enter the date, training menu, and distance swam (in km) and save it.

Q5: How can I edit or delete past records?

A5: On the "Calendar" screen, tap the relevant date to view the record. Tap the record to open the edit screen, or long-press it to bring up a deletion confirmation.

About Training Menus

Q6: How can I create original training menus?

A6: You can create one from the "+" button at the bottom right of the "Menu" screen. Build your own custom menu by adding sections like Warm-up, Main, and Cool-down, and setting the stroke, distance, number of reps, and cycle times for each.

Q7: Where can I use the menus I've created?

A7: You can select your created menus when logging a training session in the "Calendar". When you choose a menu, its total distance is automatically entered, which is very convenient.

About Goal Setting

Q8: Can I set goal times?

A8: Yes, you can set them from "Goal Times" within the "Statistics" tab on the "Calendar" screen. You can register your stroke (e.g., freestyle, breaststroke), distance, and goal time to check at any time.

About Data

Q9: Where is the data saved? Can I transfer it when I change devices?

A9: Your training records are saved locally on your smartphone. Currently, data transfer to a different smartphone (such as cloud sync) is not supported.

Other

Q10: What languages are supported?

A10: The app supports Japanese, English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, Korean, Hindi, Arabic, Bengali, Urdu, Indonesian, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Thai, and Turkish. You can switch the language from the in-app settings.
